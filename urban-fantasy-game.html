THE UNDERCURRENT
An Urban Fantasy · Chicago, 2024
"The city breathes beneath itself — sewers of intention, underground rivers of will.
Magic didn't leave America. It went underground, like everything else that mattered."
Factions
The Veil Commission An ancient bureaucracy of mages who enforce "Secrecy Protocols" — keeping magic hidden from the public. Powerful, procedural, ruthless when crossed.
The Iron Congregation A grassroots movement of urban witches and shamans who believe magic should serve communities, not be controlled by elites.
The Crimson Hand A shadow cartel trading in stolen magical artifacts and bound spirits. Run by someone called only "the Architect."
The Freeborn Circuit Idealistic hackers and activists who want to expose magic's existence to the public — believing transparency is the only path forward.
Key Characters

The Librarian (Ezra Vance) Runs a used bookshop in Wicker Park. Broker of secrets. Oldest living Veil Commission member — or claims to be.

Lore
The Undercurrent A ley line network running beneath Chicago, activated by the Great Fire of 1871. The city was rebuilt on top of it, unknowingly.
Secrecy Protocols Memory-erasure sigils embedded in city infrastructure. You've always ignored them — you've always been different.
